About

Scientist. Innovator. Project Leader.

I began my journey as an Electrical & Electronic Engineering graduate from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) in Bangladesh. Driven by a passion for healthcare innovation, I pursued a PhD at Florida Atlantic University, specializing in Bioengineering — focusing on point-of-care viral diagnostics for Zika, Dengue, and COVID-19.

Today, as a Sr. Development Scientist at Beckman Coulter Diagnostics, I lead cross-functional teams in assay transfer, reagent optimization, and regulatory readiness for clinical chemistry platforms — including the DxC500i. My work directly translates into products that improve patient outcomes worldwide.

Beyond bench science, I earned the PMP® certification (Dec 2025) to formalize my leadership in structured project management — bridging science, strategy, and stakeholder communication.
